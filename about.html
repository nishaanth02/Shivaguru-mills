TIME TIME


Our Managing Director Mr. P. Sambath started our business in the year 1992 as Textiles in powerloom segment with an office in Somanur. We have continuously achieved significant growth and by the year 1999, we had a daily production of 50,000 Meters per day in Powerloom Cotton Fabrics by giving Jobwork to more than 1,000 Powerlooms in and around entire Somanur and Palladam belt in Coimbatore district. We setup our first Sizing unit in the year 2000 under the name Sun Auto Sizing. Then we gradually started to expand into the Autoloom segment in the year 2000. Our Autoloom division was established as Premier Cotton Industries Pvt Ltd., in 2005 in Karumathampatti with a 10 Acre Campus with 141 Sulzer PU Looms, Three inhouse Sizing units and 48 Brand New Picanol Summum Airjet looms providing employment to over 200 people.

Our Managing Director was Instrumental in setting up the Airjet Division in 2015.We now having the highest autoloom weaving capacity in the entire Coimbatore district with in-house weaving of 189 looms(Sulzer-141 and Airjet-48) and outsourcing to about looms with an average production of 85,000 metres per day. Furthermore, we ventured into windmills as early as 2008 and have 4 MW in windmills to cater to our industrial consumption. Our Managing director are also in talks with advisors and various contractors with regard to foraying into the solar energy segment.
